Course Title : Mathematics II

Course no: MTH163

Semester: II

Nature of course: Theory + Practice

Full Marks: 80

Pass Marks: 32

Credit Hours: 3

Course Description : The course contains concepts and techniques of linear algebra. The course topics include systems of linear equations, determinants, vectors and vector spaces, eigenvalues and eigenvectors and singular value decomposition of matrix.

Course Objective : The main objective of the course is to make students familiar with the concepts and techniques in linear algebra,, solve system of linear equations with Gauss-Jordan method, to impart knowledge of vector space and subspace, eigenvalues and eigenvectors of a matrix and get the idea of diagonalization of a matrix, linear programming, Group, Rings and Fields.
